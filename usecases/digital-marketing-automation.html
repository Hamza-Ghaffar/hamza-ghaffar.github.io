The Problem

Marketing at Most Accountancy Firms is Stuck in 2010

Most UK accountancy firms still rely on word-of-mouth, a static website, and maybe an occasional LinkedIn post. The marketing partner (if they have one) juggles content, ads, and client outreach between billable hours. It does not scale.

📝 Content Drought

Blog posts take 4-6 hours each. Tax deadline reminders get forgotten. The website has not been updated since 2021.

🔍 SEO Blindspot

No keyword strategy. Competitors rank above you for "accountant near me." Technical SEO issues nobody knows how to fix.

💰 Wasted Ad Spend

Google Ads running with broad match keywords. No negative keyword list. 40% of clicks are irrelevant searches.

📧 Manual Outreach

Client emails sent one-by-one. No segmentation. No follow-up sequences. New leads sit untouched for days.

📊 No Measurement

Google Analytics installed but nobody checks it. No idea which channel brings the best clients. Decisions based on gut feeling.

🕐 No Time

Partners bill at 200+ GBP/hour. Every hour spent on marketing is revenue lost. Marketing always gets deprioritized.

Automation Modules

Every Marketing Channel, Automated

Each module runs independently but feeds data into the central pipeline. Everything connects, everything flows.

🔍

SEO Automation

AI audits your site weekly. Auto-fixes broken links, missing meta tags, and slow pages. Generates keyword-optimized content briefs and tracks ranking changes across 200+ target keywords.

⚡ 8 hrs/week → 30 min/week
✍️

AI Content Engine

Generates blog posts, tax deadline reminders, service pages, and LinkedIn posts in your firm's brand voice. Human reviews and approves, AI does 90% of the writing and formatting.

⚡ 6 hrs/post → 45 min/post
💰

PPC Optimizer

AI manages Google Ads bids every 4 hours. Auto-pauses underperforming ads, scales winners, adds negative keywords from search term reports, and generates new ad copy variations.

⚡ 60% lower cost per acquisition
📱

Social Media Scheduler

Auto-generates and schedules LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram posts from blog content. AI adapts copy for each platform. Posts at optimal engagement times.

⚡ 5 hrs/week → 20 min/week
📧

Email Campaign Automation

Behavioral triggers: new lead gets welcome sequence, tax deadline reminders auto-send, inactive clients get re-engagement campaigns. All segmented by service type and lifecycle stage.

⚡ 3x higher open rates
📊

Marketing Dashboard

All channels in one view: SEO rankings, PPC spend, email open rates, lead pipeline, and revenue attribution. Weekly PDF report auto-emailed to partners every Monday morning.

⚡ 10 tools → 1 dashboard

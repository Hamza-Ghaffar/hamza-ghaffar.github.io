The Problem

Manual Amazon Selling is a Full-Time Fire Drill

Amazon sellers spend 60-80% of their time on repetitive tasks: listing products one by one, adjusting prices manually, tracking inventory in spreadsheets, and answering the same customer questions. This doesn't scale.

⏱️ Product Listing

30-60 min per product. Manual title writing, keyword research, image tagging, category mapping. Multiply by 500+ SKUs.

📦 Inventory Chaos

Stock-outs and overselling. No real-time sync between warehouse and Amazon. Manual reorder decisions.

💰 Pricing Guesswork

Competitors change prices hourly. Manual repricing can't keep up. Lost Buy Box, lost revenue.

📋 Order Overload

Manual order processing, label printing, tracking updates. Each order = 5-8 manual steps.

💬 Support Burnout

Same 20 questions repeated daily. Returns, shipping status, product specs. All answered manually.

📊 Blind Decisions

No unified dashboard. Sales, margins, ad performance scattered across 10 different screens.
